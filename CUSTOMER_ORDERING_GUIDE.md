# Customer Ordering & Tracking Guide

Welcome to the Mama Jos online ordering platform. This guide will walk you through how to place an order, make a payment, and track your food in real-time.

---

## 1. How to Place an Order

### Step 1: Browse the Menu
1. Go to the **Menu** page.
2. Filter items by category (e.g., Starters, Main Course, Drinks).
3. Click on any item to view details, ingredients, and available modifiers.

### Step 2: Customize & Add to Cart
1. Select your preferences (e.g., "Medium Rare", "No Onions").
2. Choose your quantity and click **Add to Cart**.
3. You can review your items anytime by clicking the **Cart icon** in the top right corner.

### Step 3: Checkout
1. In your Cart, click **Proceed to Checkout**.
2. **Delivery Type:** Choose between:
   - **Dine-in:** Enter your Table Number.
   - **Takeaway:** Your order will be packed for pickup.
   - **Delivery:** Enter your delivery address.
3. **Rewards:** If you have loyalty points, you can apply them here to get discounts or free items.

### Step 4: Payment
1. Select your preferred payment method (M-Pesa, Card, or Cash on Delivery if available).
2. For M-Pesa, follow the on-screen prompts or enter your transaction code after payment.
3. Click **Confirm Order**.

---

## 2. Tracking Your Order

Once your order is confirmed, you will be redirected to the **Live Tracking** page.

### Tracking Stages
The system provides real-time updates through 6 key stages:
1.  **Order Received:** Your order is in our system and waiting for financial verification.
2.  **Order Confirmed:** Payment is verified, and the order has been sent to the kitchen.
3.  **Being Prepared:** Our chefs have started cooking your meal.
4.  **Ready for Pickup:** The food is ready! It’s either waiting for a rider or ready to be served to your table.
5.  **In Transit / Being Served:** 
    - *Delivery:* A rider is on the way to your address.
    - *Dine-in:* A waiter is bringing the food to your table.
6.  **Delivered / Served:** The final stage! Enjoy your meal.

### Interactive Map (for Delivery)
- For delivery orders, you can view the rider's progress on the interactive map located at the top of the tracking page.

### Live Notifications
- The status badges will update automatically without needing to refresh the page.
- You will see time stamps for when each stage was completed.

---

## 3. Managing Your Orders
- You can view your full order history by navigating to **My Account > Orders**.
- Re-order your favorites with a single click from the history page.
- Download digital receipts for any of your past transactions.

---

## 4. Troubleshooting
- **Order Not Updating:** Ensure you have a stable internet connection. The tracking uses live data streams.
- **Payment Issues:** If your M-Pesa prompt doesn't appear, check your phone's SIM toolkit or contact support via the **Live Chat** feature on the website.
