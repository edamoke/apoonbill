# The Spoonbill - Owner & Administrator Manual

Welcome to the **The Spoonbill** Restaurant Management System. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of how to manage your restaurant's digital operations, including menu control, staff management, and financial auditing.

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview
The **Admin Dashboard** is your central hub for real-time business intelligence.
- **Real-time Stats**: View today's total revenue, order count, and active tables.
- **Quick Links**: Access Orders, Products, and CRM sections directly.
- **Staff Activity**: Monitor which chefs, riders, and waiters are currently active.

---

## 2. Menu & Product Management
Maintaining an attractive and up-to-date menu is critical for customer satisfaction.

### Managing Categories
Navigate to **Admin > Categories** to view your streamlined menu structure (14 official categories).
- **Edit/Add Category**: Update the name, description, and visual representation.
- **Image Upload**: Click the **"Upload Image"** button to select a photo from your device.
- **AI Image Generation**: Use the **"Generate AI"** (Wand icon) button to automatically create a professional, appetizing food photo for any category using the Pollen AI engine. *Always click "Update Category" to save the generated image.*

### Managing Products
Navigate to **Admin > Products** to manage individual items.
- **Pricing**: Update prices instantly to reflect market changes.
- **Availability**: Toggle the "Active" switch to hide items that are temporarily out of stock.
- **Descriptions**: Ensure every item has a clear description of its ingredients.

---

## 3. Order Lifecycle & Fulfillment
The system provides a seamless flow from the kitchen to the customer.

### Active Order Management
Navigate to **Admin > Orders** for a real-time tracker of all ongoing orders.
- **Real-time Sync**: This page updates automatically. You don't need to refresh.
- **Staff Assignment**: Click an order to assign a specific Chef, Rider, or Waiter.

### The "Disconnect-Free" Workflow
1. **Chef**: Marks an order as **"Ready"** when cooking is complete.
2. **Notification**: The order immediately pulses on the Waiter/Rider boards.
3. **Rider/Waiter**: Clicks **"Start Trip"** or **"Pick Up"** and then **"Delivered/Served"** once finished.

### Order Audit & History
To keep the main tracker clean, all completed and delivered orders are moved to the **Order Audit** section.
- **Access**: Click the **"Order Audit"** button at the top of the Order Management page.
- **Purpose**: Review past transactions, investigate cancelled orders, and perform financial reconciliations.

---

## 4. Accounting & Financial Reports
Designed for Owners and Accountants to monitor profitability.

- **Order Approval**: Accountants can review incoming online orders before they are sent to the kitchen.
- **Sales Reports**: View breakdown of sales by category, item, and payment method (M-Pesa, Cash, Card).
- **Audit Trail**: Every order includes a complete timeline of who handled it and at what exact time.

---

## 5. Staff & Access Control
Manage your team and protect sensitive data.

- **Role-Based Access**: 
    - **Chefs**: Only see the Kitchen Display System.
    - **Waiters/Riders**: Only see the Fulfillment boards.
    - **Accountants**: See financial data and approval tools.
    - **Admins/Owners**: Have full control over the entire system.
- **User Activation**: New staff members must be "Activated" by an Admin before they can log in.

---

## 6. Daily Operations (POS)
For staff on the restaurant floor.
- **Table Management**: Open, merge, or split bills directly from the POS interface.
- **Shift Management**: Start and end shifts to track cash flow accurately.
